About Us

At Neuro Niche, we value neurodiversity as an essential aspect of humanity that needs to be intentionally designed and supported.

Founded by a late-diagnosed neurodivergent Occupational Therapist and mother of two, with global experience supporting individuals and communities across the lifespan.

When authentically supported — meaningful outcomes for all

When not embraced — harmful impacts for individuals & society
